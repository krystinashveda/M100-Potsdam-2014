Dear fellows and organisers, it is very honourable for me to be part of this team and to be able to participate in the event ahead of us! Thank you for choosing me.

So, let me finally introduce myself. My name is Krystina Shveda and I am from Belarus. That would be way too bold to call myself a data journalist so far, although I am really passionate about data-driven storytelling, visualisations and open data. Working as a journalist (read: idler) among thousands of programmers for three years led me to a point when I wanted to mix up those two professions and the two communities. The result is a Hacks/Hackers Minsk group that is pretty small but I hope to be able to develop it further. In September however, I am heading to London to start Master studies of Interactive Journalism at City University London. My nearest goal is to learn to use data tools and data understanding to create valuable and insightful stories and that is why I am eager to meet all of you and experiment together.
